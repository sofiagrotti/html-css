About the Platform

What is MindTrack?

MindTrack is a digital emotional journal designed to help people understand their feelings and mental patterns. Life moves fast, and sometimes we forget to stop and reflect on how we actually feel. MindTrack provides a simple and intuitive way to check in with yourself every day. By tracking your emotions regularly, you can identify patterns, recognize triggers, and develop healthier habits. Our goal is to make emotional awareness simple, accessible, and helpful for everyone.

Daily Mood Check

How are you feeling today?

Take a moment to check in with yourself. Select the emotion that best describes your current mood:

  • Happy
  • Calm
  • Neutral
  • Sad
  • Anxious
  • Stressed

After selecting your mood, you can also write a short note about your day.
What happened today? What made you feel this way?

Reflection Section

Take time to reflect

At the end of each day, MindTrack encourages you to reflect with simple questions such as:

What was the best moment of your day? What challenged you today? Is there something you are grateful for? What would you like to improve tomorrow?

Reflection helps build emotional awareness and promotes personal growth.
